Sci-marklets

Sci-marklets is Bookmarklets to change all the DOI links into scihub links. It performs it totally locally and if you refresh the page, the URLs are back to normal.

Installation Link

This is the Sci-marklet installation link. Just drag and drop it to your bookmark bar or right click and "add to bookmarks":
Sci-marklet
If you want to test it out, you can also directly click on the link above and the folowing DOI will be changed.

Test Link

There is an example of DOI to test it out : doi.org/help
